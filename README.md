# Communal
A fast and efficient social media platform for content aggregation. Communal is still in the planning state and is being actively worked on, but all details are subject to change at any point.

## What is it?
Communal is a website for people to get together and discuss things that interest them, as well as share content links and comment upon them. These gatherings are called "Communities". Each community is run by a group of self-designated moderators. Administrators provide them with the tools to moderate discussion, but do not actively participate. Each community can be further split into subcommunities, and a member posting content can choose if the link is relevant to the group at large, or just their small subsection. A member can subscribe to the whole community, or just a single subcommunity.

## Goals
Communal is being built with optimisation in mind. A cheaper running cost means advertising can be kept to a minimal. It is also being designed to promote sharing & communication, as we want to foster a community spirit. Finally, it has to look good.
